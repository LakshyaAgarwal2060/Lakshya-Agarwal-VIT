# Project Outcome

## SBI Risk Management Dashboard

**Author:** Lakshya
**Year:** 2026

---

## Executive Summary

Successfully designed and developed a comprehensive web-based Risk Management Dashboard for State Bank of India (SBI) to monitor and analyze critical banking risk indicators across multiple risk categories. The dashboard provides real-time visualization of key performance indicators (KPIs) for Credit Risk, Capital Risk, Liquidity Risk, Market Risk, and Operational Risk in compliance with RBI guidelines and Basel III norms.

---

## Key Deliverables

### 1. **Interactive Multi-Tab Dashboard**
Developed an 8-tab navigation system covering Overview, Credit Risk, Capital Risk, Liquidity Risk, Market Risk, Operational Risk, Performance Metrics, and Problem Areas, enabling comprehensive risk assessment at a glance.

### 2. **Data Visualization**
Implemented 20+ interactive charts using Chart.js library, including line charts for trend analysis, bar charts for comparative metrics, and doughnut charts for risk distribution, with custom threshold indicators for regulatory compliance.

### 3. **KPI Tracking System**
Integrated 12+ critical banking KPIs including Gross NPA Ratio, Net NPA Ratio, Capital Adequacy Ratio (CAR), Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR), Return on Assets (ROA), and CASA Ratio, tracked across Q1-Q3 FY 2025-26 with quarter-over-quarter comparisons.

### 4. **Alert & Reporting System**
Designed an alert mechanism to flag KPIs approaching critical thresholds (e.g., LCR stress alert) and implemented CSV export functionality for detailed risk reports.

## Project Impact

1. **Centralized Risk Monitoring:** Provides a single platform for executives and risk managers to monitor all critical risk indicators in real-time.

2. **Early Warning System:** Alert mechanisms enable proactive identification of potential risk breaches before they occur (e.g., LCR declining toward regulatory floor).

3. **Data-Driven Decision Making:** Interactive visualizations and trend analysis facilitate informed strategic decisions for risk mitigation.

4. **Regulatory Compliance:** Ensures continuous tracking of RBI and Basel III compliance metrics with visual threshold indicators.
